About Us

IslahPay is an innovative non-interest wallet built on the Haqq Blockchain platform, designed to provide affordable and accessible financial services to underserved populations. By adhering to sharia-compliant principles, leveraging blockchain technology, and focusing on inclusivity and affordability, IslahPay aims to empower individuals and communities, offering financial solutions that align with their religious beliefs and contribute to their economic well-being.

Sharia Compliance:

As a non-interest wallet, IslahPay strictly adheres to sharia-compliant principles in all its operations. It aims to offer financial products and services that are in accordance with Islamic teachings, avoiding interest-based transactions and promoting ethical and socially responsible financial practices.

Accessibility:

IslahPay aims to ensure that its financial services are accessible to individuals and communities who are traditionally excluded from mainstream banking systems. This includes people with limited financial resources, marginalized populations, and those seeking sharia-compliant financial options.

Affordability:

IslahPay is committed to providing cost-effective financial services that are affordable for its users. By minimizing fees and transaction costs, IslahPay seeks to remove barriers and make its services accessible to a wide range of individuals and businesses.

Security and Transparency:

IslahPay places a strong emphasis on security and transparency. By leveraging the Haqq Blockchain platform, it ensures that transactions are secure, tamper-proof, and transparent, instilling trust and confidence among its users.

Social Impact:

IslahPay seeks to make a positive social impact by addressing the financial needs of underserved populations. Through its services, it aims to foster economic inclusion, empower individuals, and contribute to the overall socio-economic development of communities.

Our Services

We aim to provide accessible and ethical financial solutions that promote financial inclusion, empower individuals, and contribute to the well-being of communities.

Micro-Savings

Our micro-savings service allows individuals to securely save small amounts of money over time, fostering a culture of financial discipline and building a foundation for future financial stability.

Micro-Lending

Our micro-lending service offers affordable loans to empower individuals, enabling investments in businesses and education for improved livelihoods.

Micro-Investment

Our micro-investment service allows individuals to participate in profit and loss sharing opportunities, fostering financial inclusion and growth while adhering to sharia-compliant principles.

Micro-Insurance (Takaful)

Our micro-insurance service offers affordable coverage for health, life, and property based on Takaful principles, providing essential financial protection for individuals and their families.

Qard-Hasan

Our Qard-Hasan service provides interest-free loans to individuals in need, supporting them during emergencies or financial hardships while upholding Islamic principles of financial assistance.

Green-Invest Services

Our green-invest services prioritize sustainable and eco-friendly investment opportunities, supporting projects that benefit the environment and communities, fostering a more sustainable future.
